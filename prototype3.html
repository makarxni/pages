About Dayspring International Academy

Dayspring International Academy (DIA) is a Christian international school established in January 2011 as a sister school to Morning Glory International School in Abidjan. We operate multiple campuses in the East Legon–Adjiringanor area of Accra.

Our mission is to provide basic quality education that develops future leaders through a balanced international curriculum. We emphasize not only academic excellence but also character formation – encouraging wisdom, humility and compassion in students.

Our Academic Programs

Preschool/Kindergarten

For ages 2-5 years, our preschool program combines the British curriculum with elements of the Ghanaian education system.

  • Play-based learning
  • Early literacy & numeracy
  • Social & emotional development

Primary School (Years 1-6)

Our primary program builds strong foundations in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and creativity.

  • Cambridge Lower Secondary
  • Literacy & numeracy focus
  • Science & ICT integration

Secondary School

Our secondary program prepares students for top universities worldwide through rigorous academic preparation.

  • IGCSE & A-Level programs
  • STEM and UCMAS-focused curriculum
  • University preparation

Cambridge & Edexcel Curriculum

Dayspring International Academy runs both the Cambridge Assessment International Examination and the Pearson Edexcel modules from preschool through secondary levels, preparing students for global academic success.

Admissions Process

How to Apply

1

Visit Our Campus

Schedule a tour of our facilities between 8am and 4pm on weekdays.

2

Complete Application

Submit the application form along with required documents (birth certificate, academic records).

3

Entrance Assessment

All prospective students take an entrance assessment before admission.

4

Admission Decision

The admissions committee will communicate their final decision to parents.

Admission Requirements

Key Information

  • Admission Period: July–August each year
  • Documents Needed: Birth certificate, academic records, recommendation forms
  • Assessment Fee: Required for entrance testing

Our Campuses & Facilities

Preschool/Kindergarten Campus

Trassaco, East Legon, Adjiringanor

Our early years campus features child-friendly learning spaces, outdoor play areas, and specialized facilities for young learners.

Primary Campus
